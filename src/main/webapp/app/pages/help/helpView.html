How do I create an account?

It’s very easy to create an account at DevWars. When visiting any web page on DevWars, click the “Sign Up” button at the top of the website.

What information do I need to sign up?

To sign up, all you need to do is choose a username, enter your e-mail and create a password. It’s that simple!

Can I sign up using Facebook?

Yes! You can sign up using multiple different social networks. These are:

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Google Plus
  • Reddit
  • Twitch
  • GitHub

What do you do with my details?

DevWars does not use any of your personal details. If you are concerned and do not want your information kept on our system, you can remove your details at any point, or even deactivate your account.

Can I change my username?

No, choose carefully! Once selected, your username cannot be modified.

I can’t remember my username or password

If you have forgotten either your username or password, a password reset URL will be sent to your profile.

I want to update my e-mail address

You can update your e-mail address by visiting your Account Settings. A verification email will be sent to confirm this change.

Can I connect or disconnect social networks to my account?

Yes! You can connect Facebook, Twitter, Google Plus, Reddit and Twitch by visiting the Social tab in your Account Settings. You will earn DevBits by connecting a social network to your profile. You can also very easily disconnect these networks too, in the same way that you connect them.

Can I deactivate my account?

Yes. If you no longer wish to keep your profile on DevWars, you can deactivate your account by going to your Account Settings.

Help! I think I’ve been hacked!

If you have reason to believe that your DevWars account has been compromised, please either visit our Contact page, or e-mail us at support@devwars.tv.

What are DevBits?

DevBits are the currency system used on DevWars. They can be earned through a variety of means, and spent in many different ways.

How can I earn DevBits?

There are a huge amount of ways to earn DevBits! They are earned through your participation around the site and during streams. Here is list of some of the ways you can pick up some Bits!

  • Being present in the Twitch stream during a live game
  • Participating in DevWars
  • Being helpful on the website
  • Sharing your badges and achievements
  • Answering surveys
  • Referring your friends

There are also plenty of other ways to earn yourself some DevBits, with many more planned to come in the future. We could tell you all about it, but where would the fun be if in that?

How can I spend the DevBits I’ve earned?

The shop is the marketplace to spend your hard earned Bits. Whether it’s to have a custom avatar on your profile or to change your title, the choice is yours! You can see how many DevBits you have earned by checking your profile on the Dashboard.

Can I sell my DevBits?

No. DevBits have no monetary value, and cannot be traded or sold.

I was in a live Twitch stream, but my DevBits weren’t added to my account!

Make sure that you have connected your Twitch account on DevWars. If you haven’t done this, there is no way to check whether you have been in stream or not! If your Twitch account is already connected to your DevWars account, and you believe that your DevBits aren’t correct, please contact a member of the DevWars team via the Contact page.

Alternatively, you may have attempted to try and spend your DevBits too early! DevBits earned within the DevWars Twitch stream are updated to your account every 15 minutes.

What is DevWars?

Here at DevWars we put your web development skills to the test. People of all levels are invited to participate in an exhilarating battle to create the best static HTML page they can within 1 hour. Teams are formed of 3 people, with the team's members controlling a language each - HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

What are the rules of DevWars?

The DevWars rules can be found by Visiting the Rules page. There are not many, and they are very simple.

If you think you have seen someone break the Rules, let us know! Cheaters suck!

What do I need to play DevWars?

To participate in DevWars, you will need:

  • TeamSpeak installed on your computer system
  • A Cloud9 account
  • A microphone
  • A basic understanding of English
  • An understanding of either HTML, CSS or JavaScript

How do I apply to play DevWars?

To apply for a game of DevWars, visit the Games page, and click the “Apply” button against a game that you would like to play in.

REMEMBER: MAKE SURE THAT YOU CAN DEFINITELY ATTEND THE GAMES YOU APPLY FOR! If you apply for a game but do not turn up, your XP will be penalised and your Rank will be affected!

I’ve been selected to play a game of DevWars, now what?

Well done! If you’ve been selected to play, you will receive an email informing you of this. The email will detail steps of what to do next, and when to be ready. You will receive a DevBits reward for participating in DevWars!

I’ve been accepted for a game, but I can’t attend it. How do I cancel?

If you cannot attend a game of DevWars that you have been accepted for, please let the DevWars team know as soon as possible. By forfeiting your position, you will be given an XP deduction.

What happens if I start playing a game of DevWars, but leave mid-game?

If you leave mid-game through no fault of our own and come back to the stream, you will not be penalised.

If you leave mid-game and don’t come back, this counts as a forfeit. By forfeiting, you will receive a large XP deduction. Please make sure that if you apply for a game, you will be able to play for the entire duration of it.

My teammate left mid-game! What do I do now?

We’re sorry that you’re in this situation. If this event occurs and your team loses, you will take a smaller XP deduction than if there were a full team.

What happens if I win a game of DevWars?

Congratulations! If you’ve won a game of DevWars, you will receive XP and some DevBits. Your XP will contribute to your rank, and your DevBits will help you to buy cosmetic items from the shop.

What happens if I lose a game of DevWars?

Bad luck! If you’ve lost a game of DevWars, you will lose a small amount of XP! All of your stats are recorded on your Dashboard.

I want to help moderate DevWars! How do I get involved?

We don’t currently offer applications to be a DevWars moderator. If in the future we decide that we need more moderators, we will let the community know via social media.

What is the DevWars Schedule?

You can catch DevWars every Tuesday and Saturday on our Twitch channel.

I missed the last game of DevWars? Can I still watch it?

Of course! All of our past streams are available to watch via our YouTube channel. You can find the game you missed via our Games page, where you will also find links to the finished code each team produced, and a live preview of the result.

How does the ranking system work?

To rank up your DevWars profile, you need to earn XP. Your rank will show other users how well you play in DevWars streams. You can check your rank by visiting your Dashboard.

How do I earn XP?

You earn XP through winning games of DevWars. However beware! If you lose a game of DevWars, you will lose XP!

I lost XP! Why did that happen?

If you have lost XP, there are two possibilities as to why this has happened:

  • You lost a game of DevWars
  • You forfeited a game of DevWars

If you have lost XP and neither of these events took place, then please head to the Contact page and let us know.

I don’t think my XP is correct

If you don’t believe that your XP is correct, please contact the DevWars team via the Contact page, with evidence as to why you believe this to be the case.

I’ve been banned from the DevWars Twitch stream! What happened?

If you have been banned from the Twitch channel, it was most likely that you violated the chat rules. If you feel that you have been incorrectly banned, please contact the DevWars team via the Contact page.

If I’ve been banned from the DevWars Twitch channel, will I still be able to use DevWars?

Yes. You will not be banned from the DevWars website if you have been banned from the DevWars Twitch channel. However, the same rules apply everywhere. If we find that you are also abusing the rules on the DevWars website. You will be banned from there too.

What are the DevWars Twitch chat rules?

  • Don’t spam
  • No racism
  • No advertising
  • Don’t abuse other viewers
  • Please speak English!

Help! A user is abusing me on the Twitch chat!

If a user is being abusive towards you or any other user, please report this person to one of the moderators in the channel.
